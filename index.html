About Us

Talk to My Hands is an interactive game for deaf children


Our team started this project during the coronavirus pandemic when most people were afraid to go outside of their houses and most likely those children with disability.


Children with disabilities are having a hard time learning and have some interest in learning through online classes.


This is the main concern of our team, so the team created an educational game for deaf children to have fun while learning ASL while they were in their houses.


The game adopted the detection technology to assist players with their progress. We work together to make this work for the sake of our deaf brothers and sisters.
